# 05 — Feature Breakdown

Each feature is scored against the one test: **does it improve a daily financial decision?** If not, it's cut. Priority: **P0** = the spine (no product without it), **P1** = the intelligence, **P2** = the depth.

---

## P0 — The spine

### 1. Accounts
Manage assets and liabilities across Personal and Business.
- Create/edit/archive accounts; set type, currency, liquidity flag, native balance.
- Reconcile: adjust `current_balance` directly (creates a `balance_adjustment` event).
- Account detail: current balance, its events, its trend.
- **Decision value:** balances are the ground truth for "where am I."

### 2. Events (logging)
The one high-frequency write. Everything is an Event.
- Add Event sheet: pick type → amount + currency → domain → (optional) account, category, goal, note → save. **Two taps for the common case.**
- Type-aware forms (smoking logs a count; investment logs ticker/units; transfer picks two accounts).
- Edit / delete with balance re-computation.
- Quick-add shortcuts for frequent events (coffee, cigarette, salary).
- **Decision value:** without Events there is no story and nothing for the AI to read.

### 3. Net Worth
The headline number.
- Consolidated net worth in base currency, with Personal / Business split.
- Liquidity figure (Σ liquid accounts) — the IPS's "protect liquidity" made visible.
- Trend from `net_worth_snapshots` (sparkline on Home, fuller chart on tap).
- "What changed" — delta since yesterday / last week, decomposed by what moved.
- **Decision value:** answers "where am I" in one glance.

### 4. Timeline
The heart. A feed of Events interleaved with AI insights.
- Reverse-chronological, grouped by day, infinite scroll.
- Filters: domain (Personal/Business/All), type, goal, account.
- Each row: icon, title, signed amount, account, time; tap → detail.
- AI insights, warnings, achievements appear inline as distinct cards.
- **Decision value:** answers "what changed" and gives the AI its stage.

## P1 — The intelligence (the reason Vector exists)

### 5. Daily Briefing (Home)
Not a dashboard. A briefing.
- Time-aware greeting ("Good morning").
- Net worth + today's delta.
- **One** recommendation (the single smartest move today).
- Business update, goal progress, risk alerts, upcoming payments, smoking cost, apartment progress — each a calm one-liner, expandable.
- **Decision value:** this is the first tap of the morning. It *is* the product.

### 6. The Advisor (AI CFO)
Your financial partner, on tap.
- Chat: ask anything; answers are calm, direct, data-driven, cite the IPS.
- Grounded in your real `FinancialState` (via the backend context builder).
- Entry point for Simulator and Board Meeting.
- **Decision value:** answers "what should I do" with your actual numbers.

### 7. Insights, Warnings, Forecasts, Achievements
Generated by the rules engine (instant, deterministic) and the AI (deeper).
- **Warning** the moment an Event trips an IPS rule (e.g. > 2500 RON impulse) — before any LLM call.
- **Insight**: what an Event means in context.
- **Forecast**: where the line goes (runway, goal ETA).
- **Achievement**: streaks, milestones (30 days no cigarette, net worth up N months).
- **Decision value:** turns raw events into judgment.

### 8. IPS (Investment Policy Statement)
The app's conscience, as editable rules.
- View/edit the rules; toggle active; tune params (e.g. impulse cap amount).
- The AI cites rules by name; warnings link back to the rule that fired.
- **Decision value:** the app protects you from yourself, on *your* terms.

## P2 — The depth

### 9. Goals
Apartment (2028), emergency fund, investments, company growth, quit smoking.
- Progress toward target amount / date; projected ETA at current pace.
- **Every expense can affect goals** — the app shows how a decision moves each goal.
- Quit-smoking goal is special: tracks days smoke-free, money saved vs baseline, health-adjacent framing kept financial ("a leak sealed").
- **Decision value:** connects today's decision to the long game.

### 10. Decision Simulator
One of the most important features. "Should I buy this?"
- Enter a hypothetical (amount, one-off or recurring, domain, which account).
- The AI simulates impact on: liquidity, net worth, apartment goal, investment trajectory, forecast — and gives a recommendation.
- **Visual** answer: before/after bars, the goal-date shift, a clear verdict.
- **Decision value:** the single highest-leverage "what should I do" surface.

### 11. Board Meeting
Weekly / monthly / quarterly review, run like a real board.
- The AI reviews behavior over the period: **what improved, what worsened, what should change**, plus one focus for next period.
- Immersive, sectioned, saved to `reviews` for history.
- **Decision value:** the strategic altitude — zooms out from daily noise.

### 12. Discipline Score (0–100)
Not a spending grade. A behavior grade.
- Components: consistency, investing, smoking, business growth, goal progress, impulse control, liquidity.
- Every change is **explained** ("−4: an impulse buy over your 2500 cap").
- Trend over time.
- **Decision value:** a single honest mirror of behavior, with reasons.

### 13. Upcoming Payments
From `recurring`: what's due and when, personal and business.
- Surfaced on Home; feeds liquidity forecasting.
- **Decision value:** no surprises; protects liquidity.

### 14. Smoking tracker
Because it's a financial leak in the IPS.
- One-tap log; running cost (day/month/year), projected annual cost, days-since-last, money saved since quitting.
- **Decision value:** makes an invisible leak visible in money terms.

## Explicitly out of scope (v1)
- Bank aggregation / Open Banking — schema-ready, not built.
- Receipts/attachments, OCR.
- Multi-user / sharing / accountant export.
- Web app.
- Widgets & rich notifications — v1.x, after the core proves itself.
- Any chart that doesn't drive a decision.
